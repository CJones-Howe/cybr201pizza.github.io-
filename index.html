About Pizza

Pizza is a popular Italian dish made with a flatbread base, tomato sauce, cheese, and a variety of toppings. It's loved worldwide and comes in many styles and flavors.

Popular Pizza Types

  • Margherita: Tomato, mozzarella, basil
  • Pepperoni: Tomato, cheese, and pepperoni slices
  • Veggie: Bell peppers, onions, olives, mushrooms
